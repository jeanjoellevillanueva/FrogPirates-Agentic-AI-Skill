# Coherence checklist

A package **fails** if any hard rule fails. Fix before scoring.

## Hard rules (must pass)

1. **Count lock** — Title says `7` → script spine has `7` items. `Top 10` → 10. `4 of 29` → 4 winners + mention 29.
2. **Headline lock** — Thumbnail Headline words appear in or directly imply the title (e.g. title `TOP 7 Must Buy`, headline `TOP 7`).
3. **Hook lock** — First 3 spoken beats mention the same hook as Headline (data %, list type, or fear).
4. **Price lock** — If thumbnail shows `$` or `%`, hook cites the same number (or says "verify live").
5. **No triple promise** — Title, thumbnail, and hook must not advertise three different videos (e.g. title OP-16, thumb EB-02, hook budget tips).

## Soft rules (fix if possible)

- Headline ≤ 4 words readable on mobile
- Badge ≠ Headline (different words)
- Sub-headline adds **why click**, not repeats headline
- Fear titles still end spine with opportunity (JJ bear format F)

## Common failures

| Failure | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Title "10 cards" thumb "BROKEN" | Put `10` or `UNDER $25` on thumb |
| Hook starts with backstory, no list | Open with list promise in sentence 1 |
| Package A and B same headline | Change emotion angle, not just title words |
| Sealed topic, no numbers | Add `+X%` or `$` to thumb or hook |

## Quick audit sentence

Ask: *If someone mutes the video and only sees the thumbnail for 2 seconds, do they know it's a **numbered list** about **[topic]**?*

If no → revise.
